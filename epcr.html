ePCR

An innovative electronic RT-PCR Test Tracking System

COVID-19 Pandemic has adversely impacted lives and economies globally. Real-Time-Polymerase Chain Reaction Test, most commonly-known as RT-PCR test is being widely used for the detection of Covid-19. The test has become a standard norm almost everywhere and getting a negative test result has become mandatory travel requirement for all types of travel either locally or internationally. Several medical centers and clinics who conduct these tests, require a seamless, robust & mobile solution to cater to the increasing demand for carrying out the test processes from registration till the updation of results.

Samsotech is pleased to introduce an easy-to-use digital system that will facilitate easy registration, tracking, and managing of the entire RT-PCR Testing Cycle. This system is complimented with advanced Passport/ID readers (Desktop/Mobile), Vital signs monitors, and 2D/3D barcode readers. The system is available as a standalone, mobile or as a networked solution combined with digital storage, archiving and reporting capablities.

Key Features

Book Test appointments or walk-in

Individuals can easily book appointments online and receive Email/SMS confirmations or simply walk-in for taking the test.

Speed up registration at the Test Centre(s)

Use of Passport/ID readers enables fast, accurate and automated data entry of ID information as well as auto verification of previous test records.

Easy capture of screening Checklist

Manual/automated data entry of vital parameters (e.g. Blood pressure) as well as capture of any pre-requsite information.

Record Sample information and Tagging

Manual/automated capture of details such as Patient ID, Transport Vial Information, Date and time of collection, Name and title of the medical staff, Medical Centre details etc.

Create and Print RT-PCR Test Certificate

Manual/automated update of test result information, and Email/SMS/Print RT-PCR Test Certificate.
