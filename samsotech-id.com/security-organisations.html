Security Organisations

Several large organizations need to enter identification details of all their visitors, contractors and employees into their computer systems at their entrance gates. Additionally, they are required to fill in personal contact details along with entry and exit timings. Some corporations also use this information to print a visitor pass. Our Systems automate this process by scanning and reading of passports, driver licenses, ID cards, etc.

We have certified interfaces with several leading access control systems worldwide. For more information on interfaces, we offer, please contact us.

It is ideally suited for Security Companies, Lobby and Access Control, Banks, Mortgage Companies, Insurance, Companies, Foreign Exchanges, and Money Transfer Bureaus.
