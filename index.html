About Us

Connecting technologies, innovation, and business

Samsotech is a leading global developer and supplier of innovative IT solutions to a variety of industries. A pioneer in hospitality technology and hotel guest facing solutions, Samsotech has proudly implemented over 9,480+ installations in 141+ countries. We help organizations find greater success on their digital transformation journey in a multi-dimensional way. Our solutions empower our customers with a competitive edge and gives them the flexibility in how they connect, interact, and transact with their customers. We also focus on delivering innovative, & customized solutions that can satisfy hotel or hotel chain specific requirements as well as country-specific local regulatory requirements. All our hospitality products and solutions come with a certified PMS interfaces and web-Interfaces. We are an Oracle Gold Partner and also work with all leading PMS systems globally.
