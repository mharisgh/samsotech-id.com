SamsoSmile

SamsoSmile is a product conceptualized by a dentist and developed by engineers. It helps dental offices reduce their write off % by increasing accuracy of patient benefits and eligibility.

  • Empower your team with a full benefit breakdown directly from the payer, in less than 60 seconds.
  • Spend lesser time on the phone and more on direct patient care.
  • Search through code- specific limitations and downgrades. Estimate patient portion with greater accuracy!

How to use SamsoSmile

  1. Plug in patient and practice information.
  2. Hit 'Check Eligibility'
  3. Get real-time details on maximums, deductible, coinsurance and limitations.
  4. Take a print out or save as PDF in your Practice Management System. Information also gets saved in 'Search History' on the cloud.
